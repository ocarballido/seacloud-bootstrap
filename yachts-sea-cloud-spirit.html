SEA CLOUD SPIRIT

Ranked among best luxury passenger ships in the world, the SEA CLOUD SPIRIT redefines the elite experience of being on board and close to the sea. Her design was guided by that of the original SEA CLOUD, with the addition of 21st century technologies that modernized the fleet in 2021. The newest and largest of the SEA CLOUDS welcomes up to 136 guests, with plenty of space to let your spirit soar and explore. This authentic three-mast ship with 28 sails hoisted by hand is flooded with light, state-of-the-art luxuries, and ultimate relaxation.

TECHNICAL DATA AND SAIL DIAGRAM

The SEA CLOUD SPIRIT is built as a three-mast full-rigged ship. A tallship is called a full-rigged ship when it has at least three masts with complete square rigging.

You can easily recognize them by the sails that are attached at right angles to the ship. Three masts have been built since the 15th century. At that time, they usually had square sails on the first two masts and a triangular lateen sail on the last mast – so they were not yet full ships. The SEA CLOUD SPIRIT will be technically state-of-the-art. In addition to 44,100 square feet of sail area, she has two economical, environmentally friendly diesel electric motors.
