All

Decked Out

Invigorate the senses with the sun warming your skin and the salty sea air filling your soul. This is what yacht sailing is all about. Take time to chill on the spacious sun deck, then hit the lido for alfresco cocktails, where a pianist entertains during early evening and accompanies after-dinner drinks. End the night under starry skies on the ocean. On our original SEA CLOUD, this happens at the stern of the ship’s Blue Lagoon, a comfy outdoor retreat classic for onboard relaxing.

The
Fine Dining

Experience

Your culinary journey aboard the SEA CLOUDS is most impressive at the four-course evening meals. In our upscale restaurant filled with light from panoramic ocean views, a full-service experience brings guests together for one intimate, sophisticated seating. Combining classic, modern, and maritime elements, chefs complement the menus with regional specialties, often procured in port that same day, or caught directly from the back of the boat. Wines reflect the regions visited throughout the voyage.

Library

& Longe
Retreats

Shared spaces on the SEA CLOUDS add to the historic allure. Stylish to the very last detail, the light-filled library and upscale lounge invite you to linger anytime day or night. On exclusive Cultivated Journeys, this is where expert guest speakers captivate the room with informative talks, or up-and-coming talents from the Steinway Artist family entertain on the legendary pianos.
