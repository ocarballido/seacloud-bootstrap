2024
final farewell

Experience Northern Europe and her sunny Atlantic coasts, totally relaxed aboard our luxury sailing yacht, SEA CLOUD SPIRIT. Join us for a final farewell next summer, when the region reveals its legendary treasures along wild and romantic coasts with blooming gardens, famous forts, charming fishing villages, gourmet delights, and more.

8-Night Taste of the Atlantic Coast

Bilbao to Honfleur

May 28 to June 5, 2024

Savor, sip and sail elite epicurean regions, enjoying Basque Country tapas and exquisite seafood dishes in Aquitaine, Brittany and Normandy. In the world's wine capital, Bordeaux delivers its best vintages, while casks from La Rochelle give a taste of Cognac and Pineau. Sommelier Nils Lackner shares insights, equipped with a champagne saber and decanter.

8-Night Culinary Summer for Connoisseurs

Edinburgh to Hamburg

June 27 to July 5, 2024

Sail into a spirited summer inspired by Nordic cuisine, from the time-honored distilleries of Scotland to the Michelin-star restaurants of Sylt. Sip single malts from Speyside, try Norwegian wild herbs, eat fresh North Sea prawn and aromatic oysters on this culinary sailing adventure.

10-Night Luxury Lifestyle & Relaxing Beats

Hamburg to Bilbao

July 13 to July 23, 2024

As part of our signature Curated Moments — exclusive events beyond the sea — we treat you to a special culinary evening in the wine metropolis of Haut-Médoc. "La vie est belle" on summer nights in Bordeaux. Then sail to San Sebastián, the Basque Country port calling to gourmets from around the world.
