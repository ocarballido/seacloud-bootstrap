Mediterranean

& ATLANTIC COAST

Smaller ships, bigger experiences—that’s how the we explore Europe’s secret ports. And the prestige of yachting aboard the SEA CLOUDS means rare access to luxuries like docking in Old Town Venice as well as evening time and overnights—for dining and nightlife without the big ship crowds. Our luxury yachts are smaller and more exclusive so you can get closer to the destinations for new experiences in the Old World.

Spain, Portugal, Morocco

From sunny days on the famous beaches of Spain to magical nights in Morocco, see it all in spring or fall. Unique Old Towns mix with modern city flair along these exotic coasts. Eat paella in Valencia, dance flamenco in Andalusia, and shop the sophisticated streets of Mallorca. In the Canary Islands, landscapes range from desert to forest, with the Teide volcano in Tenerife as the highest peak. Traverse craters and lava rivers, explore cloud forests, and embrace Las Palmas’ tranquility. And Portugal calls with seafood culture on the mainland and the Azores’ lush eternal spring.

French
Riviera

In the south of Europe, savor French wine and caviar along the Côte d’Azur. The western part of the region offers a rich tapestry of cultures, and includes high-end stops at St. Tropez, Monte Carlo, and all around Corsica.

Greece, Italy, Sicily, Croatia

Sail east to discover Greek antiquities, Italian treasures, and baroque Adriatic splendor. In the legendary Aegean light, discover the charming harbors and island villages large ships can’t access. With a brilliant blue-and-white backdrop, explore olive groves, old monasteries, and ancient sites across the Peloponnese and the Cyclades.

In Venice, where the nobility immortalized themselves with art and architecture, enjoy rare access to SEA CLOUD luxuries like docking in Old Town, a privilege not available to mainstream cruise ships. Or circumnavigate Sicily’s hideaway bays and the majesty of Mount Etna. Ancient temples, theaters, and charming towns stun with unique character only available to bijoux yachts and smaller ships.

And the Adriatic proudly shows it has more than one pearl to offer from the pine forests of Istria and the waterfalls of the national park to the cathedral in Sibenik. Often we have long evening layovers for the luxury of extra time in Croatia’s great cities.
