SEA CLOUD 2

With its own prestigious personality, the SEA CLOUD II marries the modern luxuries of a private yacht with the classic romance of a tall sailing ship. Built in 2001 with the maritime family resemblance of the original SEA CLOUD, this second generation welcomes up to 94 guests with expansive spaces to unwind and reset, exclusive outside staterooms, and pampering from a 65-member crew waiting to tend to every whim — all of which earned it a Berlitz five-star rating.

TECHNICAL DATA AND SAIL DIAGRAM

Since 2001, the SEA CLOUD II, with a total length of 384 feet, 23 sails and about 32,000 square feet of sail area as a three-mast barque, has also enriched the fleet of blue-water cruise ships under sails.

The use of the most modern materials, from metal for both the frames and masts to new types of plastic for sails and ropes, makes it possible to achieve almost the same sailing characteristics as a four-masted barque by using larger dimensions for the masts and sails with only two masts rigged on the frame and one gaff rigged mast. Among experts, the SEA CLOUD II would only be referred to as a “barque”, because only if the ship has more than three masts does one have to additionally specify the number.
