Discover the sea cloud difference

Since 1979, the SEA CLOUDS have been one of the world's most legendary names in sailing, offering the ultimate opulence at sea. With three authentic tall ships — sails still hoisted by hand — the luxury fleet combines maritime tradition with attention to detail and white-glove service for the refined sophistication of a private yacht. In harmony with nature, the SEA CLOUDS veer away from the crowds, sailing to exclusive destinations with only 64 to 136 guests.

The Ship

Sea cloud spirit

She modernized the fleet in 2021 and welcomes 136 guests for an elite hotel experience on board, tended to by an 85-member crew. The authentic sailing ship is flooded with light, luxuries and ultimate relaxation.

The Ship

Sea cloud II

Built in 2001, with a family resemblance to the original SEA CLOUD, this 2nd generation welcomes 94 guests with expansive decks, outside suites and staterooms, and pampering from a 65-member crew.

The Ship

Sea cloud

The 1931 private sailing yacht of Marjorie Merriweather Post and Edward Hutton leads our luxury fleet, with every detail radiating the glamour of a bygone era for 64 guests and a crew of 60.

Boutique

Hotels at Sea

Plush suites and deluxe staterooms are serene retreats at sea, with artful interiors, marble bathrooms with golden fixtures, and small sitting areas.

Gourmet

Side of sailing

Refined culinary creations complemented by fine wines and spirits take center stage on the SEA CLOUDS. Award-winning chefs, sommeliers, and champagne experts curate epicurean experiences at sea.

Sea cloud

Inclusives

Extra comforts and exclusive experiences are all-included when sailing the luxury of the SEA CLOUDS. Enjoy a welcome bottle of champagne, daily fruit basket in suites and staterooms, open bar, gratuities, water sports, and more.

Curated Moments

Private events in port come complimentary as part of the SEA CLOUD Difference. Think Steinway piano concerts, after hours at opera houses and museums, culinary vineyard visits, or front-row regatta and racing seats.

Distinctive Destinations

The SEA CLOUDS sail to the world’s most beautiful places, where upscale local charm welcomes discerning guests, devoid of crowds. Every itinerary is hand selected and special, so you can experience the world in a different way.

Sustainability

For more than 40 years, responsible tourism at sea and on shore has guided our philosophy of sustainability. With smaller ships and fewer guests, we create more meaningful experiences with less impact left behind.

Celebrating

Commemorate special days in upscale ways on a SEA CLOUD sailing yacht. From birthdays and anniversaries to weddings and honeymoons, we curate exclusive onboard experiences to celebrate life's most cherished moments.
