Sustainability

Pure

Sailing

Don’t miss the thrill of pure sailing when our crew release the sails by hand. Wind-power sustainability is a SEA CLOUDS tradition, embracing gentle tourism.

As one of the only luxury fleets to still hoist the sails by hand, we relish every opportunity to synchronize with the wind, the sea, and the endless horizon, in harmony with nature’s rhythm.

Wind power

Sailors scale upwards of 190 feet into the shrouds, release the heavy cloth, then vigorously tighten the ropes down on deck. There are no buttons that can simply be pressed: It takes almost a full hour until all sails catch the wind. This time-honored tradition uses full wind power for 30 to 50 percent of any given sailing. When not riding the winds, the ships are powered by diesel-electric engines with low-sulfur marine diesel fuel.
